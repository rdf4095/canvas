ShapeCanvas class
    - nudge size should probably use scale() instead of coords(). Coords() acts
    on the bounding box of the shape object, and so it will change the shape of the
    object, if the ratio of horizontal to vertical size is not accounted for.
    The change in shape will be gradual (it's one pixel at a time), and will only
    become apparent after many nudge actions.
    - Gradually changing the shape, i.e. changing the short axis of an ellipse (oval),
    may be an effect you want. If so, it should probably be a separate control,
    and not a "nudge for size", which assumes both axes are changed symmetrically.
    - When using scale(), 1.01 and 0.99 (1% at a time) may not be enough of a change per
    nudge. Test this to see if it is enough.
    - Inceasing the scale factors will quickly result in large changes to the object
    with each nudge. this suggests that you cculd implement "coarse" and "fine"
    adjustments, say with Ctrl-arrowKey and Shift-Ctrl-arrowKey respectively.
